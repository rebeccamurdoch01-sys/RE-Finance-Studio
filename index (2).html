About the Studio

Supportive Finance, Backed by Experience

With almost 10 years of experience in finance across a range of industries, I created RE Finance Studio to provide modern, approachable finance services for creative and event businesses.

As an AAT Licensed Accountant and part-qualified CIMA professional, my aim is to help businesses feel more confident in their finances through clear communication, reliable guidance and a more personal experience.

Having worked within the events industry, I understand that event finances can often be fast-paced and unpredictable compared to more traditional businesses. My approach focuses on bringing clarity and structure to the financial side of your business, in a way that feels practical and easy to understand.
